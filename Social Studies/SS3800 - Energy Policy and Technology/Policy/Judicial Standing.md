- What cases the courts will and will not do
- Requires 3 Things:
	1. Injury in-fact
		- Legitimate harm was caused
	2. causation 
		- Something has been proven to have caused said harm
	3. redressability
		- The court can do something about it
### Prudential Standing
- Courts can only do things that the judicial branch can do. If it's an executive/legislative thing, then they can't do it. They can only do court stuff.
