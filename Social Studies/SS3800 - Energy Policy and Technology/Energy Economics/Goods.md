## Good Types
- Rivalrous - Using it diminishes supply for others
- Excludable - People can be prevented from using it

- Private (Rivalrous + Excludable)
	- "Hey, can I use your water heater?" "No lmao"
- Common (Rivalrous + Non-Excludable)
	- Ends up getting drained
- Club (Non-rivalrous + excludable)
- Public (Non-rivalrous + Non-Excludable, to a degree)
