- Social Costs
- situations when the production/consumption of goods and services creates costs/benifits for others which aren't reflected in the prices charged for the goods and services
- Example - Oil Extraction sites are decrepit, but the cities that use the oil are super wealthy

## Ways to Deal With Externalitites
### Pigouvian Approach
- You break it, you buy it
- Whoever causes it pays for it (polluter pays)
### Coesian Approach
