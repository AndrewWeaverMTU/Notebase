- The flow of an electric field through an object
- Outward = Positive, Inward = Negative, no net charge = no net flux

$$\huge \Phi_E = \oint \vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A}$$
### How We Handwave
#### Parallel Planes
 $\huge \Phi_E = \oint \vec{E} \cdot d\vec{A} = \int EdA = E \int dA = EA$
#### Non-Parallel Planes
$\huge \Phi_E = \oint \vec{E} \cdot d\vec{A} = \int E \cos(\theta)dA = E\cos(\theta)\int dA = EA\cos(\theta)$
- Where $\theta$ is the angle between the planes.
- Taking the component of $\vec{E}$ normal to the surface
