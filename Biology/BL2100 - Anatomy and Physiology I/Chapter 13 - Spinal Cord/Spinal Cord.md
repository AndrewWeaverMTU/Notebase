- Part of the [[Central Nervous System]]

- Contains reflex circuits to control rapid responses
- [[Gray Matter]] is a site for integration of [[Synapse#^122112|Postsynaptic Potentials]]

### Damage to Spinal Cord
- “Transection” - partial/complete severance of the spinal cord
	- Paralysis WILL occur, degree depends on location
		- C1-C3: neckdown
		- C6-C7: some arm and chest muscles
		- T10-L1: Most thigh muscles
		- L1-L2: Most Leg muscles
