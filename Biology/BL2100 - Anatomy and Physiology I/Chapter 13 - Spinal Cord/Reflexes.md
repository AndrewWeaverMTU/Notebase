## Spinal Reflexes
- Signal is processed and integrated in the [[Spinal Cord]], not the brain
- Can be monosynaptic or polysynaptic
	- monosynaptic - one [[Neurons|neuron]] in the [[Central Nervous System]] ^76e0d5
	- polysynaptic - multiple neurons ^311aaf
