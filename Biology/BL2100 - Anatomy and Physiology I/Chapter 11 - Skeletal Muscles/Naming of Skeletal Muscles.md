 - [[Skeletal Muscle|Skeletal Muscles]] are named based on their distinctive features.
 - Could be named based on origin, number of origins/insertions, size, shape, and/or location. 
 - Almost always includes the origin
## Naming Conventions
### Direction
- Recuts - Parallel to midline
- Transverse - Perpendicular to midline
- Oblique - Diagonal to midline

### Size
- Maximus - largest
- Minimums - smallest
- Longus - Long
- Brevis - Short
- Latissimus - Widest
- Longissimus - Longest
- Magnus - Large
- Major - Larger
- Minor - Smaller
- Vastus - Huge

### Shape
- Deltoid - Triangula
- Trapezius - Trapizoid
- Serratus - Saw-toothed
- Thomboid - Diamond-Shaped
- Orbiculais - Circular
- Pectinate - comblike
- Piriformis - Pear-shaped
- Platys - Flat
- Quadratic - Square, 4 sided
- Gracious - Slender

### Actions
- Flexor - Decreases joint angle (flexes)
- Extensor - Increases joint angle (extends)
- Abductor - Abducts
- Adductor - Adducts
- Levator - Raises body part (levitation)
- Depresser - Lowers body part (depression)
- Supination - Turns palm anteriorly (so you can hold soup)
- Pronation - Turns palm posteriorly (so you can’t’ hold soup)
- Sphincter -Decreases the size of an opening ^bbbaef
- Tensor - Makes a poly part rigid
- Rotator - Rotates a bond around its longitudinal axis
