- Innermost layer of the [[Eyes]]
- Lines the posterior 3/4 of the eye.

## **Sensory Layer**
- (Neural Layer)
- Multilayered outgrowth of the brain that processes visual data.
3 Distinct sublayers, superficial -> deep
### **Photoreceptor Cell Layer**
- Contains the rods and cones
	- Rods allow dim light vision
	- Cones allow bright light color vision
		- Red, Green, Blue cones
### **Bipolar Cell Layer**
- Middle relay point between photoreceptors and ganglion cells

### **Ganglion Cell Layer**
- Take the sensory information and transmit it to the [[Optic (II) Nerve]]
