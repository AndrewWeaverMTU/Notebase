## Abdominal
- **Celiac Plexus** (Solar Plexus)
	- Innervate stomach, spleen, pancreas, liver, gallbladder, kidneys, adrenal medullae, testes, and ovaries
	- Everywhere it hurts to get hit
- **Superior Mesenteric Plexus**
	- Small and large intestines
- **Inferior Mesenteric Plexus**
	- Large intestine
- **Hypogastric Plexus**
	- Pelvic viscera
- **Renal Plexus**
	- Renal arteries in kidneys and uterus
