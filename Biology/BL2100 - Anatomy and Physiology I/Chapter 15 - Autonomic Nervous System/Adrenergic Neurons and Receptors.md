### Adrenergic Neurons
- Adrenergic [[Neurons]] release [[Norepinephrine]] (noradrenaline)
	- Most sympathetic [[Postganglionic Nerurons]]


### Adrenergic Receptors
- Alpha Receptors
	- a1 - excitatory
	- a2 - inhibitory
- Beta Receptors
	- B1 - Excitation
	- B2 - inhibition
	- B3 - thermogenesis (heat production), found in brown adipose tissue (not found in adults generally)
