## Pathways from Sympathetic Trunk to Visceral Effectors
- Spinal Nerves
	- Axons exit via intervertebral foramen & connect to smooth muscles and sweat glands
- Cephalic Periarterial Nerves
	- Extend to the head & innervate parotid salivary glands
- Sympathetic Nerves
	- Innervate heart and lungs. Increase heart rate, dilate airways to lungs.
