- Produces [[Red Blood Cells]] in a process called hemopoiesis (blood making)
- Made up of developing blood cells, [[adipocytes]], [[fibroblasts]], and [[macrophages]] 
- found in developing fetal bones and in some adult bones
	- [[Sternum]]
	- [[Vertebrae]]
	- [[Skull]]
	- [[Humerus]], at the ends
	- [[Femur]], at the ends
