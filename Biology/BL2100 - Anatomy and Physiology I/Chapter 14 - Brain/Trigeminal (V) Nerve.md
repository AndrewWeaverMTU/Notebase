- **Largest cranial nerve**
- has 2 roots on the anterolateral (front outside) surface of [[The Pons]]
- carry touch, pain, and thermal signals.
- Has three major branches:

### Opthalmic Nerve
- smallest branch
 - passes through the [[Superior Orbital Fissure]]
- goes to the eye
- Sensory axons for the upper eyelid, [[Cornea]], lacrimal glands, upper nasal cavity, side of the nose, forehead, and anterior half of the scalp
	- Whole ass sinus area is innervated by the opthalmic
### Maxillary Nerve
- Goes to the jaw ([[Maxilla]])
- Passes through the [[Foramen Rotundum]]
- Sensory axons from the nose, palate, pharynx, upper teeth, and lower eyelid
	- Top mouth and bottom eye and nose.

- Largest branch
