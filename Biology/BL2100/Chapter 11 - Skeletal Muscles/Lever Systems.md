- 3 Parts:
	- Fulcrum
		- The pivot point.
	- Effort
		- The force that causes the movement
	- Load
		- Weight on the lever, opposing the movement
		- The thing actually being moved by the lever
	
### First Class Levers
- Fulcrum is between effort and load (EFL)
- Like a teeter totter
- Body Examples:
	- Head resting on the vertebral column

### Second Class Levers
- Load is between fulcrum and effort (ELF)
- Like a wheelbarrow
- Body Example:
	- Standing up on your toes
- 

### Third Class Levers
- Effort is between fulcrum and load (FEL)
- Body Example:
	- Biceps brachii - pulls up on your forearm past the elbow joint
