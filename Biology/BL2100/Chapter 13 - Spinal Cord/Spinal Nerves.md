- Mixed nerves
	- posterior roots have sensory axons
	- anterior roots have motor axons
### Location of Spinal Vertebrae
- Cervical (C1-C8) - exit above corresponding vertebrae (except c8, which exits above T1)
- Thoracic (T1-T12) - exit below corresponding vertebrae
- Lumbar (L1-L5) - Part of the [[Cauda Equina]]
- Sacral (S1-S5) -  Part of the [[Cauda Equina]]
- Coccygeal (Co1) Part of the [[Cauda Equina]]

## Branches of Spinal Nerves
- Called **rami**
- 2 Major rami
	- ### Posterior (dorsal) ramus
		- travel to deep muscles and skin on posterior of trunk
	- ### Anterior (ventral) ramus
		- Travel to skin on anterior and lateral trunk, muscles of trunk and limbs.
	

### Nerve Connective Tissue
- Epineurium surrounds the entire nerve
- Perineurium around the nerve fascicle
- Endoneurium around each [[Nerve Fibers|Nerve Fiber]] and [[Myelination of Neurons|Myelin Sheath]]
