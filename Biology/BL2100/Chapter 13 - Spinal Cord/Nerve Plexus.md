- Functional groups of [[Spinal Nerves]]
### Cervical Plexus
- Supplies the skin and muscles of the head, neck, top of shoulders and chest, and diaphragm
- C1-C5
	- Lesser occipital
	- Great Auricular
	- As a Cervicalis
	- Transverse Cervical
	- Supraclavicular Nerve
	- Phrenic Nerve
		- Ennervates the diaphragm
### Brachial Plexus
- Provides the nerve supply to the shoulders and upper limbs
- Subunits:
	- Roots, Trunks, Anterior and Posterior Division, Cords, Branches
- C5-T1
	- Musculocutaneous Nerve
		-  Coracobrachialis + Biceps Brachii + Brachialis
	- Axillary Nerve
	- Median Nerve
		- Forearm Flexors
	- Radial Nerve
		- Triceps Brachii + Extensor Muscles of forearm
	- Ulnar Nerve
### Lumbar Plexus
- Suppiliies anterolateral abdominal wall, external genitals, and parts of the lower limbs
- L1-L4
	- Iliohypogastric
	- Iliioinguinal 
	- Genitofemoral
	- Lateral Femoral Cutaneous
	- Femoral
		- Quadriceps femoris group
	- Obtuator
		- Thigh adductors and gracilis
### Sacral Plexus
- L4-S4
	- Superior Gluteal
	- Inferior Gluteal
	- Sciatic Nerve:
		- Common fibular Nerve
			- Tibialis anterior
		- Tibial Nerve
			- Gastrocnemius and soleus
	- Posterior Cutaneous nerve of thigh
	- Pudendal Nerve
