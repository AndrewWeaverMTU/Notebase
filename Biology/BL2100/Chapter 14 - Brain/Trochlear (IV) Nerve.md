![[Oculomotor (III) Nerve#^3835e6]]
- (trochle - pulley)
- Smallest [[Cranial Nerves|Cranial Nerve]]. 
- Comes from the posterior part of the [[Brain Stem]]
- Somatic motor neurons originate in the Trochlear Nucleus in the [[Mesencephalon]]
- Wraps around [[The Pons]] and exits through he [[Superior Orbital Fissure]]

- Supplies the [[Superior Oblique]] muscle of the eyeball.
