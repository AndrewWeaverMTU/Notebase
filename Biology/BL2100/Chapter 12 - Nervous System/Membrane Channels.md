
### Leak Channels
- Alternate between open and closed
	- "Random"
- K+ channels are more numerous than Na+ channels
- Found in nearly all cells

### Ligand-Gated Channels

^b98729

- Respond to chemical stimuli (ligand binds to receptor -> Channel Opens)
- Found in dendrites of some sensory [[Neurons]]

### Mechanically Gated Channels

^50a7dc

- Respond to mechanical vibration or pressure stimuli
- Found in dentrites of some sensory [[Neurons]]

### Voltage-Gated Channels
- Respond to direct changes in [[Membrane Potential]] 
- Found in axons of all types of [[Neurons]]
