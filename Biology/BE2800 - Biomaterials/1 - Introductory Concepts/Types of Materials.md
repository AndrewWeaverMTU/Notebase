### Metals
- [[Material Strength|Strong]], [[Material Ductility|Ductile]]
- High thermal and electrical conductivities
- Opaque, reflective
### Polymers/Plastics
- Compounds of non-metallic elements
- [[Material Hardness|Soft]], [[Material Ductility|ductile]], low [[Material Strength|strengths]], low densities
- Low thermal & Electrical Conductivities
- Opaque, translucent, or transparent.
### Ceramics
- Compounds of metallic and non-metallic elements
- [[Material Hardness|Hard]], [[Material Ductility|Brittle]] (not ductile)
- Low thermal and electrical conductivities
- Opaque, translucent
