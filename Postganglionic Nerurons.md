- Exit the [[Autonomic Ganglia]] and innervate organs


## Sympathetic
- four main ways of connection to [[Preganglionic Neurons]] 
	1. Synapses in the ganglion
	2. Travel to a higher or lower ganglion in the trunk then synapse
	3. Travel to a prevertebral ganglion and synapse there
	4. Synapse with chromafin (adrenal) cells instead.

## Parasympathetic
- Synapse near a visceral effector
